LOUISE'S FLORIST — where the photos go
======================================

Save Louise's flower photos from her Facebook page into THIS folder,
named exactly like this (JPG):

  hero.jpg   -> big background photo behind the "Beautiful flowers" heading
  g1.jpg     -> gallery: hand-tied bouquets
  g2.jpg     -> gallery: seasonal blooms
  g3.jpg     -> gallery: bright & cheerful
  g4.jpg     -> gallery: wedding flowers
  g5.jpg     -> gallery: sympathy tributes
  g6.jpg     -> gallery: gift arrangements

How to get them from Facebook (facebook.com/Louisesfloristhoddesdon):
  - Open a photo, right-click it -> "Save image as..." -> save into this folder
    with the right name above.
  - Or take screenshots and crop them.

Then just refresh the website (index.html) — the photos appear automatically.
Until a photo is added, a tasteful colour tile shows in its place (never a
broken image).

Tip: roughly square photos work best for the gallery; a wide/landscape one
looks best for hero.jpg.
